About The Event

AlgoXNirmaan is a 36-hour hackathon where creativity meets technology. Organized by the Vectorix Community, this event brings together talented individuals to collaborate, innovate, and build solutions to real-world problems. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your tech journey, AlgoXNirmaan offers a platform to showcase your skills, learn from industry experts, and win exciting prizes.

This year's hackathon features multiple tracks including AI/ML, Blockchain, IoT, and more. We also have a special Capture The Flag (CTF) competition for cybersecurity enthusiasts. Join us for an action-packed weekend of coding, learning, and networking!

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the hackathon

Who can participate in AlgoXNirmaan?

AlgoXNirmaan is open to students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts of all skill levels. You can participate as an individual or form a team of up to 4 members.

Do I need to have coding experience?

While coding experience is helpful, we welcome participants with diverse skills. Teams typically need a mix of developers, designers, and domain experts. We also have mentors to help you throughout the event.

Is there a registration fee?

No, AlgoXNirmaan is completely free to attend. We believe in making tech events accessible to everyone passionate about innovation and problem-solving.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

You should bring your laptop, charger, any hardware you might need for your project, and a valid ID. We'll provide food, drinks, and a comfortable workspace throughout the event.

Will accommodation be provided?

We'll provide comfortable resting areas at the venue for participants who want to take short breaks. For those coming from out of town, we have partnered with nearby hotels to offer discounted rates.

Can I work on a pre-existing project?

No, all projects must be built from scratch during the hackathon. However, you can come prepared with ideas and discuss them with your team beforehand.

How does the judging work?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, technical complexity, usability, presentation, and relevance to the chosen track. Our panel of judges includes industry experts, tech leaders, and representatives from our sponsor companies.
