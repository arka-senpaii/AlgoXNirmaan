MISSION PARAMETERS

Algo-X-Nirman is a 30-hour national-level hackathon organized by the Vectorix Community. It's a high-energy innovation sprint where tech enthusiasts come together to build cutting-edge solutions to real-world challenges. From brainstorming to prototyping, this event celebrates the spirit of creation and collaboration.

The hackathon aims to foster innovation, nurture problem-solving skills, and encourage participants to step beyond conventional boundaries. It provides a platform for bright minds to explore emerging technologies, push their limits, and transform bold ideas into impactful solutions.

Algo-X-Nirman is open to students, developers, and tech enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds. Whether you're a seasoned coder or a first-time hacker with a passion for innovation, this event welcomes all who are eager to learn, create, and compete in a dynamic and inclusive environment.
